Strategy Guide
4 strategies · click any tab to learn how it works
📈 S1 — RSI Momentum (Classic)
The original strategy from your repo. Uses RSI to detect momentum breakouts on NSE/BSE equities and index options. RSI > 60 = bullish momentum; RSI < 40 = weakness. Battle-tested on Nifty 50 during morning sessions.
🟢 BUY — RSI crosses above 60
Enter long at market. Trailing SL activates at entry price minus SL %.
🔵 Recovery BUY — RSI crosses above 42
After a SELL, if RSI dips below 40 then recovers above 42 — re-enter. Lower-risk post-dip entry.
🔴 SELL — RSI drops below 40 or SL hit
Exit position at market. Whichever condition fires first.

💡 Best for: Trending Nifty 50 stocks during morning sessions (9:30–11:30). Avoid sideways/choppy markets.
📊 S2 — EMA(44) + Bollinger Bands
Trend + volatility combo. EMA(44) confirms the trend direction; Bollinger Bands identify price extremes and squeeze breakouts. Together they find high-probability mean-reversion entries within established trends.
🟢 BUY — Price above EMA(44) AND touches Lower BB, then reclaims
Uptrend confirmed + pull-back to value. Wait for price to close back above lower band.
🔴 SELL — Price touches Upper BB OR crosses below EMA(44)
Overbought extension or trend reversal. Exit whichever fires first.
💡 Best for: Options (NIFTY CE/PE) and mid-cap Nifty stocks. EMA(44) aligns with weekly swing moves.
⚡ S3 — MACD Crossover
MACD (Moving Average Convergence/Divergence) measures momentum via EMA difference. When the MACD line crosses the signal line, it signals a momentum shift. Classic trend-following suited to medium-term moves on both equities and index options.
🟢 BUY — MACD line crosses above Signal line with positive histogram
Bullish momentum building. Histogram turning green and widening is a stronger signal.
🔴 SELL — MACD line crosses below Signal line
Bearish momentum. Exit long position at market immediately.
💡 Best for: 15m and hourly charts. BANKNIFTY futures and large-cap Nifty stocks with strong volume.
🎯 S4 — RSI + MACD Combined
The strongest signal filter — both RSI AND MACD must agree before any trade is taken. Fewer signals but much higher accuracy. Ideal for options trading where being wrong is costly.
🟢 BUY — RSI > 55 AND MACD bullish crossover simultaneously
Both momentum indicators confirm — very high probability entry. Wait for both conditions before executing.
🔴 SELL — RSI < 45 OR MACD bearish crossover
Either indicator flipping bearish is enough to exit. Protect profits fast.
💡 Best for: NIFTY/BANKNIFTY weekly options. High conviction trades only. Expect 20–30% fewer signals vs S1, with 10–15% higher accuracy.
